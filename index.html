// About Tamec

From the Sky to the Bedrock.

Tamec Logistics & Consultancy is a multi-disciplinary mining and logistics company operating at the intersection of engineering, technology, and workforce solutions. We bring together mining engineering expertise, drone-powered aerial exploration, and precision blasting operations under one roof.

From consultancy in mining engineering and blasting operations to rockdrill machine fitting and specialized blasting training, we equip extraction operations with the skills, equipment, and intelligence they need to perform. Our drone technology enables mining teams to survey, plan, and monitor sites with a level of precision that ground-based methods can't match — while our logistics and labour broking services keep every operation staffed and supplied.
